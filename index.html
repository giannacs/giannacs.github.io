Model Parameter Estimation with Maximum Likelihood

I am passionate about statistics. Maximum likelihood estimation is a branch of statistics that is sometimes poorly motivated when taught in higher education. This project is an attempt at showing all the mathematical steps that lead to the maximum likelihood estimation method

  • Worked example of how maximum likelihood approach is used when estimating model parameters
  • Full formalism behind the worked example, explained step-by-step

Financial Modeling - Compound Interest Calculator

Outside of my research in Physics, I am passionate about understanding and modeling financial markets. Most recently, I built my own compound interest calculator from first principles. I noticed that a lot of the calculator online lack tranparency in their calculations and can be perceived as fairly limited to a financially educated person. This custom-built calculator is meant to be used by such person. Since this project's completion, I have been exploring the impact of home-building on the broader housing market using a Bayesian Statistics approach. Stay tuned for more updates on that!

  • Custom and Interactive Compound Interest Calculator in Python
  • All-in-one adjustable options for retirement planning
  • Educational Content with all methods used

Simulation of Cosmic Microwave Background Signals

You might wonder what the signal from the first light of our Universe looks like when measure by the most sensitive detectors on the planet. In this project I explore exactly that! I simulate the universe at that time using existing cosmic microwave background (CMB) simulation packages in Python, and sample it with detectors

  • Simulate time-ordered data from a single realization of the temperature and polarization signal of our Universe 380,000 years after its birth
  • Realistic noise simulation
  • Using realistic curved sjy projection

Notes on Mutlivariate Linear Regression

One of the biggest challenges in an analytical field is converting a real-world problem into a mathematical one. This can be achieved with a mathematical parametric model. To extract information about the model one can use the process of linear regression. It is especially relevant in the field of statistics, machine learning, etc. The aim of this project is to illustrate the power of linear regression

  • Linear regression for pedestrians
  • With some linear algebra, get a powerful estimating tool
